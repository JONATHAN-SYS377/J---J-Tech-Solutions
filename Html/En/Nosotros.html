About J&J TechSolutions

Our History

J&J TechSolutions was founded in 2022 by Jonathan Sequeira Cespedes and Julio Hurtado Ortiz, graduates of INA as Computer Application Developers, both professionals passionate about software development, committed to providing tailor-made technological solutions for our clients.

We specialize in the creation, development, and implementation of web applications, mobile applications, websites, as well as the sale of technological components, maintenance and repair of computers, and the installation of security and surveillance cameras.

Vision

To be recognized nationally and internationally as a leading company in providing services and platforms that facilitate the digital transformation of organizations.

Mission

Provide high-quality service that exceeds the expectations and needs of our clients, delivered by an exceptional staff committed and passionate about the values of J&J TechSolutions, aiming to achieve a positive impact in all our works.
